LABS

Growth Accelerator

Adaptech LABS is a new venture capital fund making resources and guidance from experienced industry mentors available for early-stage startups interested in developing and rapidly scaling their business, using a leading-edge approach.

Grow Your Startup With Us

Our program is available to help startup businesses that are interested in building solutions in an event-sourced way, but need to bring together more resources to get their business to the next stages.

Adaptech LABS is a Venture Capital fund taking on 10 new businesses (and growing) each year, with the goal of supporting and accelerating them through all stages of their growth from startup to enterprise acquisition.

We are uniquely partnered with key service providers and funding providers that have a fundamental understanding of the core approach to scale business and technology together. If you are an investor and would like to share in the success of rapidly growing, vetted, and guided startups, click here.
